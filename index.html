ABOUT US

Ricks' Pizza was founded in the late 90s by the late Ricky Mwethera. After his trip to italy, the food culture he saw there inspired him to bring that food culture back to his home country ,Kenya. He and two of his friends started as a small establishment and braced through the tough times, to get where we are today. Our pizza is considered as the closest you can get to having a full italian culture and through the authenticity is how we got to be one of the top in East Africa.
