ABOUT US

Ricks Pizza is an establishment that was built from scratch by the Founders

  1. Ricky Mwethera

  2. Hellen Mwakitu
It was founded in the year 2010 after the co-founder (Ricky Mwethera) took a trip to italy and after seeing and very much enjoying the food culture in Italy, he thought of the idea that people in his home country would love since then. What can i do to make Kenyans be able to experience this culture? . That was the beginning of the organisation that has now grown to be recognised as the closest experience to the Italian culture.
